Journal Entry #10: Constructing my Device

Developing my product, September 30, 2018

I have at last started constructing my product and have completed a majority of it. Throughout the process, I have gained some valuable experience working with electrical components and have developed a stronger understanding of how electrical components work and function in an electric device. I learned how electrical components are placed onto breadboards, gaining a fundamental understanding of the positive and negative legs of a breadboard. I first attached the 2200uF capacitor, followed by some jumper wires, and then connected the 100uF capacitor. Then I connected the voltage regulator, and finally I soldered jumper wires onto the solar panel (with the guidance of my Dad) and connected it to the breadboard. I could go into further explanation of the construction process of my project, but I will save that for my report, where I will go into further depth about the design of this project. My next step is to splice a USB cable and connect it to the breadboard, and then I will test my product and see if it is successful.
