Journal Entry #8: Voltage Regulators Research

Learning about Voltage Regulators! September 22, 2018

Today I did some research on voltage regulators and learned about its purpose in a circuit and how it works. I then created a simple schematic (diagram) of a voltage regulator using AUTOCAD 2016. This diagram will be attached below. The voltage regulator I will be using for my project is an LM7805 voltage regulator. An LM7805 voltage regulator outputs a positive voltage of 5V. A voltage regulator with LM78XX has a positive voltage, and the last two numbers is the voltage output. A voltage regulator with LM79XX has a negative voltage. Voltage regulators are necessary in circuits where a consistent amount of DC voltage is needed throughout a circuit. In a phone charger, the voltage needs to be consistent because a phone can only consume a certain amount of voltage. An inconsistent voltage output or a voltage output that is too high can short circuit a phone and ruin it. A typical phone requires about a 5V output voltage, which is why I am using a 5V voltage regulator for my circuit.
