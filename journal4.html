Journal Entry #4: Some Stuff Came in the Mail Today

Parts that have arrived, August 12, 2018

Some materials I ordered for my personal project came today. There is nothing much to use to start the project right away, but I may experiment with some the the parts that came. A quick overview of the parts:

Breadboard - The electric board which I will construct my product on

Jumper Wires - Wires that will provide path for the electricity to travel

Voltage Regulator - To regulate the voltage and make sure the voltage does not go over 5V, or else phone would short circuit and fry up

That is all for today! I am going to try experimenting with these products, and test out the voltage regulator, just to make sure it regulates the voltage to 5V. Hopefully the rest of my parts will arrive soon.
